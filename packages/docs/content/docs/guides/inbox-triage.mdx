The inbox is the pull-request list, but it is not sorted by date. It is sorted by
**what needs doing today** — grouped into sections, loudest first. Reading it well
is how you decide what to open next.

Press `g` then `i` to reach the inbox from anywhere.

![The inbox: pull requests grouped into Waiting on you, To review, Drafts in progress, and Everything else, each row showing repo, branches, labels, and an unresolved count on the right](/assets/screenshots/inbox.png)

## The sections

The list breaks into sections, ordered by urgency:

| Section | What it holds |
|---|---|
| **Waiting on you** | PRs you authored that have unresolved threads waiting on your reply. The loudest section. |
| **To review** | PRs assigned to you or otherwise needing your review, each with its unresolved-thread count. |
| **Drafts in progress** | PRs where you have a saved local draft you have not submitted yet. |
| **Everything else** | Open PRs that do not currently need anything from you. |

## The loudest number on screen

Each row shows an **unresolved count** on the right. The unresolved count on your
own PRs is deliberately the loudest number on the screen: it is the work that is
blocking someone else. When a row in *Waiting on you* shows a count, that is a
thread a reviewer left that you have not answered — open it and
[walk the queue](/docs/guides/author-queue).

Each row also carries the repo, the base and head branches, labels, and how long
ago it was updated — enough to decide whether to open it without syncing.

## What the inbox costs

The inbox is the one surface that keeps polling while it is open. It refreshes
against GitHub's PR list with a conditional request, so an unchanged list comes
back as a free `304` and costs nothing against the rate budget. Opening a PR is
what triggers a [sync](/docs/guides/first-sync) — the expensive part — so triage
is cheap and deliberate: you read the inbox, then choose what to pull down. See
[the rate budget](/docs/concepts/rate-budget) for the full accounting.

## A triage pass

1. Press `g` `i`.
2. Clear **Waiting on you** first — those threads block other people.
3. Work **To review**, highest unresolved counts first.
4. Resume anything in **Drafts in progress** you meant to finish.
5. Open a PR with `↵` to [sync and review it](/docs/guides/first-sync).
